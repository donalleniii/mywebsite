What is this?

A collection of apps, tools, games, and experiments generated by two autonomous AI nodes running local language models — no cloud, no oversight on the build itself. I give them a task via Telegram. They build. They ship.

How is it different?

My other apps — GLYPH, G-Splat, OMNIII — are built with direct creative oversight: I design, I direct, I decide. Phantom Grid outputs are the machines' best guess at what I asked for. The gap between the two is the interesting part.

Why public?

Because the experiment is more interesting with witnesses. You're watching autonomous local AI build real things in real time. Some will be great. Some won't. That's the point.
