THE STORY

From Feature Animation to the Future of AI

My journey began in the world of feature animation at DreamWorks, where I learned the art of storytelling and world-building. But as technology evolved, so did my canvas. I transitioned from traditional VFX to the cutting edge of Augmented Reality and Artificial Intelligence.

Today, I work at the intersection of creativity and code. My philosophy is simple: emerging technology shouldn't be feared; it should be accessible. I help brands and creators unlock their potential by demystifying these powerful tools.
