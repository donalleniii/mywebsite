Support

Having trouble? Here are the most common questions and how to get help.

Quick tip: Most issues are solved by making sure you have a valid API key entered in Settings and that your device is connected to the internet for cloud AI features.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

Open OMNIII, grant microphone access, and tap the record button. Your voice is transcribed on-device. To use cloud AI features, go to Settings and enter your OpenRouter API key.

Where do I get an API key?

Sign up at openrouter.ai, add credits, and copy your API key. Paste it into OMNIII Settings. Your key is stored securely in the iOS Keychain — it never leaves your device except to authenticate with OpenRouter.

Why did my AI response fail?

Common causes: expired or invalid API key, insufficient OpenRouter credits, or no internet connection. Check your key in Settings and verify your credit balance at openrouter.ai/credits.

Is my audio sent to the cloud?

No. Audio is transcribed on-device using WhisperKit. Only the resulting text is sent to AI services when you explicitly request a response.

How does OMNIII Consensus work?

OMNIII Consensus sends your transcript to multiple AI models in parallel, then synthesizes the best answer from all responses. This gives you a more balanced, well-rounded result than any single model alone.

Can I use OMNIII completely offline?

Not yet — but it's on the roadmap. On-device local language models and vision capabilities are currently in development and may be available in a future update. For now, OMNIII requires an internet connection for AI responses.

How do I restore my Pro purchase?

Open OMNIII Settings and tap "Restore Purchases." This uses Apple's StoreKit to verify your subscription. No OMNIII account is needed.

How do I delete my data?

Swipe left on any memo in History to delete it. To remove all data, uninstall the app — everything is stored locally and will be permanently erased.
